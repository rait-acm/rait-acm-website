About Us

RAIT ACM women chapter focuses on

  • Providing resources, seminars, and workshops to enhance technical skills and knowledge of women in technology.
  • Promoting networking opportunities for women in the field of technology and computing.
  • Creating a supportive community that fosters mentorship, collaboration, and growth for women in technology.

RAIT ACM women chapter, a part of an internationally known organisation, Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), is a new beginning for all ages of women in tech. Supporting and celebrating all women with the motivation and helpful resources, RAIT ACM-W set into action on 9th of February 2023 and is going ahead headstrong.

VISION

The vision of the RAIT ACM Women Chapter is to create a community of women in technology who are empowered, supported, and inspired to achieve their goals. The chapter aims to promote gender diversity and inclusivity in the field of computer science by providing a platform for women to come together, share their experiences, and learn from each other. We strive to encourage and support women to pursue careers in technology by providing mentorship, opportunities and more. Ultimately, the RAIT ACM Women Chapter envision "Cognizance for All".

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter?

    RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter is a community within the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) that focuses on supporting and advancing women in computing. It provides a platform for networking, mentorship, and professional development opportunities.

  • How can I be part of RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter?

    There are several ways to get involved with RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter. You can join RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter by joining the official ACM student chapter. You can also participate in RAIT-ACM-W events, conferences, and workshops. Additionally, you can contribute to RAIT-ACM Women's initiatives by volunteering.

  • Is RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter only for women in computer science?

    While RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter primarily focuses on supporting women in computing, it welcomes participation from anyone who shares its goals and values. Men can also contribute to the community as allies and supporters.

  • What are the benefits of joining RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter?

    Joining RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter provides numerous benefits, such as access to a supportive network of professionals, mentorship opportunities, career guidance, and resources for professional development. It also offers a platform to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to initiatives that promote diversity and inclusion in the computing field.

  • How can I contribute to RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter initiatives?

    There are several ways to contribute to RAIT-ACM Women’s Chapter initiatives, such as volunteering for committees, proposing and organizing events, writing articles and blogs, or sharing your expertise and experiences through speaking engagements.
