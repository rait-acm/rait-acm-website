ABOUT TECHNOTHON

The Biggest Tech Event

Technothon is a platform for all those talented students, developers, and engineers, to compete over 24 hours to find creative solutions to solve tough and real-world problems related to focusing on performance. The coding competition inspires developers to keep their focus aside from pure functionality and bring innovativeness to the concept.

  • Registration Dates: 13th October - 22nd October 2023
  • Team Size: 4 members
  • Free registrations
  • Participants who advance past round 1 will be subject to a fee of 500 INR
  • Cash prizes will be announced soon!

Final Round : November 17 2023

Registration Criteria

1. Only UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS can participate in TECHNOTHON.
2. This competition is open for all irrespective of their branch, year of study etc.
3. Once the participants have registered for the event , they have to meet the requirements for ROUND 1.
4. Only students who will pass round 1 of the competition will be eligible for final round.
5. Students can work in teams where each team would have 4 members mandatorily.
6. Submissions should be made on time. Late submissions will not be taken into account.
