ABOUT US
ACM-W is Association for Computing Machinery for Women,a collective effort within the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) to support and empower women in the field of computing. ACM is an international professional organization dedicated to advance the field of computer science and information technology. ACM Women aims to address the gender gap and promote diversity and inclusion in technology-related fields.

FAQ'S

+What are the key dates for registration in the international level Hackathon?

Registration for the Hackathon begins on 5th November 2023 and closes on 28th November 2023.

+Are there any specific eligibility criteria for participants in this competition?

Yes, participants must be at least 16 years old, and this Hackathon is open to all students, but only women are allowed to register.

+Can I participate individually, and what is the allowed team size?

No, solo entries are not allowed. Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 members and can have a maximum of 4 members.

+How are participants selected to advance in the competition?

They are required to create a video presentation where they explain the content of the PPT.

+What are the benefits of taking part in this Hackathon?

Participants will receive valuable certificates for their resumes, and the competition promotes teamwork, skill development, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

+What is the primary focus of the National level Coding Competition Hackathon?

This Hackathon primarily focuses on showcasing the coding skills and problem-solving abilities of talented programmers from across the nation.

+Who is eligible to participate in the competition in terms of gender and academic background?

The competition is open to all female students, regardless of their branch or year of study.

+Is there a registration fee, and what are the prizes for the winners?

Registration for the Hackathon is free, and the prize pool for the winners is 35,000 INR.

+Is the Hackathon a 24-hour event, and where will it be conducted?

It is a 24-hour Online hackathon.
